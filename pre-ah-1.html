The Pre-AH model:
 Avoidable Hospitalization


The PreAH algorithm takes into account claims from Inpatient, Physician, Pharmacy, as well as demographics and environmental factors to create a unique predictive model that gives risk scores, reasons for risk, and even a timeline built into the technology.

PreAH provides an intelligence and early warning system to assist healthcare workers in preventing major events with proactive care measures.

The Impact on preventable ER visits:

Pre-AH is PreSquared’s flagship predictive model,
helping guide the care for over two million covered lives every day.

Pre-AH uses advanced machine learning algorithms to predict each beneficiary's likelihood of experiencing an avoidable inpatient hospitalization or emergency department visit during the next 30 days, giving providers the risk rankings they need to accurately maximize the value of their care. Our risk scores are used across the provider spectrum, from primary care physicians and nurse managers to care coordinators and front office managers. By “working the list”, providing relevant interventions to the highest-risk beneficiaries first, they are able to improve quality rankings, increase their patient’s quality of life, and decrease total cost of care.

Our models are trusted by providers and their staff because of their high predictive power and relevance for each specific beneficiary cohort. For example, we have versions of the model trained separately for Medicare and Medicaid beneficiaries. These populations exhibit unique characteristics and risk profiles and thus warrant their own unique risk scoring algorithms. We know how important it is to help the right person and the right time, and we put in the work to make that happen.
