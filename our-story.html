Our Story

PreSquared was founded by university faculty with one mission: provide access to proven, cutting-edge machine learning technology to the provider community to enhance the efficacy of their care interventions. 

Our patent-pending models have been in production informatics environments for years, garnering praise from providers, care coordinators, payers, and policymakers. The feedback we have received so far has been amazing, with the vast majority of users consistently leveraging the tools throughout the year. We have deployed through health information exchanges, through managed care entities, and directly to provider groups, providing our risk scores to those individuals who can intervene to prevent adverse events.

We have heard providers call our algorithm the “crystal ball” and “like magic” because of their ability to accurately risk rank a patient cohort, and consistently identify the most relevant risk factors related to their risk. PreSqaured’s footprint continues to grow, with access to a growing library of predictive algorithms, so that you can help the people who need it the most.
